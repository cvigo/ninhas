VERB PAST TENSE PAST PART. MEANING EXAMPLE (Suggested)
To achieve achieved achieved To successfully reach a goal or result I didn't achieve the goal.
To behave behaved behaved To act in a particular way... Does he behave at school?
To respond responded responded To reply or react to something We are responding to the question.
To speak spoke spoken To say words to communicate by talking You aren't going to speak in the project.
To support supported supported To help or give assistance... They supported the team at the game.
To learn learnt / learned learnt / learned To gain knowledge or skills... Did she learn me new things?
To expect expected expected To think that something will happen Will we expect a storm?
To provide provided provided To give or supply something needed I was providing her the opportunity.
