Instructions: Complete this short test about should, must, and have to. Pay attention to whether the sentence is a suggestion, a rule, or a prohibition.

Section 1: Fill in the Blanks

Use: should, mustn't, have to, or don't have to.

1. You touch the exhibits in the museum. It is strictly forbidden.
2. I think you drink more water; it is good for your health.
3. At our school, we wear a uniform. It's a mandatory rule.
4. You bring a coat today. It is very hot outside!
5. "I have a lot of homework." — "You start doing it now."

Section 2: Multiple Choice

Circle the correct option (a, b, or c).

6. You _____ play football in the living room. You might break something!
a) shouldn't    b) don't have to    c) have to
7. To get a good grade, students _____ study regularly.
a) mustn't    b) should    c) don't have to
8. In the library, you _____ shout. You need to be quiet.
a) shouldn't    b) mustn't    c) don't have to
9. It's Saturday! I _____ go to bed early tonight.
a) have to    b) must    c) don't have to
10. Before eating, you _____ wash your hands.
a) should    b) don't have to    c) shouldn't

Section 3: Sentence Transformation

Rewrite the sentences using the word in brackets.

11. It's a good idea to be kind to others. (SHOULD)
You
12. Running in the hospital is prohibited. (MUSTN'T)
You
13. It's not necessary to help me with my homework. (DON'T HAVE TO)
You
14. It is a school rule to arrive before 9:00 AM. (HAVE TO)
We
15. My advice is to sleep 8 hours a night. (SHOULD)
You

Solucionario (Teacher's Key)

1. mustn't: Prohibición estricta.
2. should: Consejo de salud.
3. have to / must: Regla escolar oficial.
4. don't have to: Falta de necesidad (es opcional/innecesario).
5. should: Recomendación lógica.
6. a) shouldn't: Consejo para evitar un accidente doméstico.
7. b) should: Recomendación para el éxito académico.
8. b) mustn't: Prohibición en un lugar público regido por normas.
9. c) don't have to: No hay obligación por ser fin de semana.
10. a) should: Hábito higiénico recomendado.
11. You should be kind to others.
12. You mustn't run in the hospital.
13. You don't have to help me with my homework.
14. We have to arrive before 9:00 AM.
15. You should sleep 8 hours a night.
